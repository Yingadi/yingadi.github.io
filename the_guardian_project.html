This is our final project for the specialization course. From October to December, we designed the game concept, mechanics, menus, levels, and all other game-related elements, compiling everything into the GDD. From January to late March, we developed the first level out of five, including sprites, tiles, animations, and programming.

The game is set in a medieval world ruled by an order that protects villagers from a mysterious evil. According to the prophecy, every 100 years, a chosen one will be born to defeat the evil forever. They are taken when they are nearly ten years old, as this is when their power is at its peak. Now, a new chosen one is ready to fulfill the prophecy, and you are a guardian knight with the mission to escort the child to the sacred place, protecting them from any danger.

Technical Details: This game is a platform-metroidvania game with a 2D pixel art aesthetic. It was developed using the unity 2D engine and features exploration and puzzle mechanics, as well as combat with different types of enemies

My role: I was responsible for designing the level layout of zone 5, creating the tileset with another partner for all zones (which my teammates used in their areas), and programming the logic for the attack mechanics and their animations (though I did not create the animations themselves).
